CORONA OR NOT !! Knowing The Differences

  1. dry cough + sneeze = Air polution
  2. cough + mucus + sneeze + runny nose = Common cold
  3. cough + mucus + sneeze + runny nose + body ache + weakness = Flu
  4. cough + mucus + sneeze + runny nose + body ache + weakness + high fever + difficulty breathing = Corona Virus

Note: provided by pathology department AIIMS, Delhi.
